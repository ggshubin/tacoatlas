TacoAtlas Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 19, 2026

TacoAtlas is a personal taco (and burrito / torta) logging app. Your data stays yours. This policy explains what is collected, why, and how it is handled.

1. Who We Are

TacoAtlas is operated by George (contact: mographguy@gmail.com). References to "we", "us", or "our" mean TacoAtlas.

2. Data We Collect

2a. Data You Provide

  • Taco spot names, addresses, ratings, and notes you log in the app
  • Photos you choose to attach to a review
  • Email address (for account creation and sign-in via Supabase Auth)

2b. Data Collected Automatically

  • Approximate location — used only when you tap "Find My Tacos" to search for nearby spots via the Google Places API. Location is not stored on our servers.
  • Device identifiers — RevenueCat may log an anonymous install ID to verify your Pro purchase across reinstalls. No personally identifiable data is tied to this ID.

2c. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Precise GPS coordinates stored on our servers
  • Contacts, messages, browsing history, or any data unrelated to the app
  • Health or financial data

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To store and sync your taco reviews across devices (Supabase)
  • To find nearby taco spots when you request it (Google Places API)
  • To verify and restore your Pro purchase (RevenueCat)
  • To operate your account and respond to support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

TacoAtlas uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — cloud database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Google Places API — nearby business search. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Review data is stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. If you enable cloud sync (Pro), your data is also stored in Supabase on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

6. Data Retention

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is removed from our servers within 30 days.

7. Account Deletion

You can request account deletion at any time using the form below. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Request Account Deletion →

8. Children's Privacy

TacoAtlas is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, email mographguy@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions? Email mographguy@gmail.com.
