Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to make the app work. We don't sell your data. Guest users' data never leaves their device. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

This Privacy Policy explains how TacoAtlas ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the TacoAtlas mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

If you use the app as a guest (no account), all data — spot locations, reviews, photos — is stored only on your device. Nothing is transmitted to our servers.

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information — your email address and, optionally, a display name and username you choose
  • Profile data — optional fields such as a profile photo, bio, home city, and favorite taco
  • Spot and review data — the locations, ratings, notes, and photos you log, subject to your privacy settings
  • Device information — push notification token (only if you grant permission), used solely to send you notifications you request
  • Purchase records — if you purchase TacoAtlas Pro, the transaction is handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. We receive only a confirmation of your entitlement; we do not store payment card details

Location data — the App uses your device location to center the map and to attach coordinates to spots you log. Location is used on-device and in the data you explicitly choose to save; we do not track your location in the background.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's core features (map, reviews, social features)
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in
  • To send push notifications you have opted into (e.g. friend requests)
  • To respond to support requests you send us
  • To improve the App based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns

We do not use your data to serve advertising and we do not build advertising profiles.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With other TacoAtlas users — according to your privacy settings. Reviews and profile information you mark as "Public" or "Friends" are visible to the corresponding audience within the App.
  • With service providers — we use Supabase (database and authentication) and RevenueCat (purchase validation). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies and security standards.
  • As required by law — we may disclose information if required by a valid legal process, court order, or government authority.

4. Your Privacy Controls

Within the App you can:

  • Set each review to Private, Friends only, or Public
  • Control whether your profile is publicly discoverable
  • Control whether your display name is shown to other users or only your @username
  • Revoke push notification permission at any time in your device settings

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time by contacting us at hello@tacoatlas.app. Upon deletion we will permanently remove your account, profile, reviews, and associated data from our servers within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Guest data is stored only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear its storage.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, including encrypted connections (TLS), row-level security policies on our database, and secure authentication via Supabase Auth. No system is perfectly secure; in the event of a breach affecting your data we will notify you as required by applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

TacoAtlas is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps Platform — for map display and location search. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — for database, storage, and authentication. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — for in-app purchase management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were most recently made.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, contact us at hello@tacoatlas.app.
